About Anandwan

A community of hope and healing

Anandwan ("Forest of Joy") was founded in 1949 by social activist Baba Amte as a self-sufficient community for leprosy patients and other disadvantaged people who were rejected by mainstream society.

For over 70 years, we have been providing medical treatment, rehabilitation, and social integration to leprosy patients, enabling them to live with dignity and become self-reliant.

Our Approach to Leprosy Treatment

At Anandwan, we follow a holistic approach to leprosy treatment that includes:

Early Detection

Proactive screening and community education to identify cases early.

Multi-Drug Therapy

WHO-recommended drug combinations for effective treatment.

Prevention of Disabilities

Regular nerve assessments and early intervention.

Rehabilitation

Physical therapy and assistive devices for those with disabilities.
